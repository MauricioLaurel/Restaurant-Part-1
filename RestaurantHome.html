Mauricio's Michelin Star Restaurant is a burgeoning culinary powerhouse located on the scenic East Hastings street in Vancouver. Chef Laurel has honed his skills watching cooking shows on both Netflix and Youtube over the last five weeks. He specializes in a type of cuisine that would be best described as 'edible' by the harsh denizens of East Hastings. While the Michelin stars are imaginary, Mauricio Laurel's microwave is not. There are very few people in the Greater-Vancouver region who can press the power button with as much zeal and passion as chef Laurel.
